# Sample Email Format You'll Receive

## Email Header:
```
From: Web3Forms <noreply@web3forms.com>
Reply-To: visitor@email.com (the person who filled the form)
To: yasirsohail191491@gmail.com
Subject: New Contact from Portfolio
```

## Email Body:
```
You have received a new message from your website contact form.

Name: John Smith
Email: john.smith@company.com

Message:
Hi Yasir,

I came across your portfolio and I'm impressed with your work on the 
Savari Transportation Platform. We're looking for a Lead Backend Developer 
for our startup.

Would you be available for a discussion?

Best regards,
John

---
This email was sent from your website contact form.
Powered by Web3Forms
```

## Reply Options:
- You can **reply directly** to the email
- The Reply-To address will be the visitor's email (john.smith@company.com)
- So you can just hit "Reply" in Gmail to respond to them

---

## Email Notifications:
- ✅ Instant delivery (within seconds)
- ✅ Direct to your inbox
- ✅ Reply-To automatically set to visitor's email
- ✅ Subject line customizable in api/contact.js
- ✅ No spam (Web3Forms has good deliverability)
